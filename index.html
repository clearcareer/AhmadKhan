What Does Ahmad Do?

I am an undergraduate student at York University studying Philosophy. I am currently pursuing a career as a Lawyer to advise and deliver legal services (such as incorporation, foundational agreements, intellectual property protection, or preparation for financing rounds) to entrepreneurs and management teams who are building technology driven companies.

I have a passion for community building initiatives.
